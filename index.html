How to Play

Welcome to Emoji Cascade! Drop emoji blocks into the grid. Align four identical emojis in a row, column, or diagonal to score points. As you score, the levels increase, making the game faster and more challenging. Use the on-screen buttons or arrow keys to move the falling emoji left, right, or down. Special emojis appear occasionally—match them to unleash powerful effects!
